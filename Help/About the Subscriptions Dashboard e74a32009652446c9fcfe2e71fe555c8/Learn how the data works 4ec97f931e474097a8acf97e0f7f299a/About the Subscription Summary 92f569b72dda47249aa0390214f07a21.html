About the Subscription Summary

If you need to understand the current situation of your subscriptions, the summary brings together all the information you need to make decisions.

It also includes purchases with intelligent installment and recovery, so you can understand all recurring sales in one place.

  • We provide both the static and dynamic status of each subscription. Understand what the static and dynamic statuses are. This way, you can understand, for example, whether the active subscription is new, a renewal, or even a reactivated subscription. You can also find out which canceled subscriptions were canceled in the last billing cycle or before.
  • You can find out if the billing is monthly, yearly, or another type. This makes it easier to study the behavior of monthly subscriptions separately from annual ones, for example.
  • You can find out if the subscription is in a trial period and when it ends.

All of this is followed by the buyer's contact information so you can get in touch with them.

Various data analyses are possible based on the summary. Take data-driven actions to boost your business!

Subscription statusIndicates the Subscription’s current status. Inactive: The first recurrence hasn’t had its payment confirmed/approved, and is overdue.  Active: The payment of the last recurrence is up to date and the subscription is still in effect. OVERDUE: The subscription period is over. The recurrences charged in this period may be all paid up or some may be overdue.
