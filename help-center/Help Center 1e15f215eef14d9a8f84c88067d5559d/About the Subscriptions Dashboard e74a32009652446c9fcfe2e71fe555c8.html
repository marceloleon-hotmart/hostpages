About the Subscriptions Dashboard

Analyze the complete journey of your subscriptions with the Subscriptions Dashboard.

Through intelligent filters, it's possible to study isolated periods and gain valuable insights for each product, offer, and plan type, such as monthly and annual plans. In the History, we use a new type of subscription status focused on how they behave over time. This way, month by month, you can analyze the moments of best performance. Discover trends in delinquent, renewed, and canceled subscriptions to improve your strategy and achieve better results. You can also view and export subscription and transaction lists to create your own custom visualizations.
